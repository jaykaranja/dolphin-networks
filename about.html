About

Healing emotional wounds, building resilience, and empowering individuals, families, and organizations to thrive through trauma-informed coaching and education.

  1. Home
  2. About

Your Guide to Healing & Growth – Monica Muiruri

I’ve lived through the emotional chaos of childhood trauma—and emerged with a mission to help others do the same. My work is grounded in compassion, clarity, and real transformation.

  • Helping individuals and teams heal from emotional wounds and unresolved trauma.
  • Guiding leaders, parents, and professionals toward emotional intelligence and resilience.
  • Creating safe spaces in homes, schools, and workplaces through trauma-informed practices.

As a mediator and trauma-informed trainer, I go upstream to uncover the root causes of pain, conflict, and disconnection. Together, we can break harmful cycles and build healthier, more connected communities.
